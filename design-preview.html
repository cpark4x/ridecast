Design Principles
Simple. Warm. Effortless.
One job per screen. No configuration. Complexity is invisible — the surface is obvious.
Neutrals carry the UI.
Off-white canvas, white surfaces, gray text. Color appears only to direct attention or signal state.
One accent. Used with restraint.
Amber-orange is the only brand color. It appears on primary CTAs, active states, and episode artwork. Never decorative.
Artwork is the emotion.
The gradient carries warmth and energy. Chrome recedes. Content leads.
