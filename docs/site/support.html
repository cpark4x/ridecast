Frequently Asked Questions

What content formats are supported?

Ridecast accepts URLs (web pages, articles), PDFs, Word documents, text files, and plain text you paste directly.

How long does it take to generate an episode?

Most episodes are ready within 1-3 minutes, depending on the length of the source material.

Can I listen offline?

Yes. Download any episode to your device and listen without an internet connection.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at chris.park@gmail.com and we'll delete your account and all associated data.

Is my content private?

Yes. Your submitted content and generated episodes are associated with your account and not shared with other users. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
