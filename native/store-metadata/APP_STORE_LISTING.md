## Description
Ridecast transforms any content into produced AI audio episodes for your commute. Drop in a URL, PDF, or document and get back a professionally narrated audio episode — complete with natural conversation flow and expert commentary.

**How it works:**
1. Share a link, paste text, or upload a document
2. AI analyzes the content and writes an engaging audio script
3. Premium text-to-speech brings it to life
4. Listen on your commute with full playback controls

**Features:**
- Background audio playback with lock screen controls
- Offline listening — download episodes for your commute
- Library management — organize your audio episodes
- Multiple content sources — URLs, PDFs, documents, text
- Dark mode support
- Secure authentication via Clerk

**Perfect for:**
- Commuters who want to learn on the go
- Busy professionals who prefer listening to reading
- Anyone who wants their reading list in audio form
